AprilTag Image Layout

Upload an image, move the four AprilTags, resize tags or the image stage by dragging their edges, and download the final overlaid image.

PNG is the default because it keeps AprilTags and dashed lines sharp. For photos, JPEG or WebP usually creates a much smaller file.

Tag: drag from the middle to move; drag an edge or corner to resize.
Image stage: drag the image area to move the whole stage; drag the stage edge or corner to resize the preview. The export keeps the original image resolution.

Line connection rule:
Top: TL top-right → TR top-left
Right: TR bottom-right → BR top-right
Bottom: BR bottom-left → BL bottom-right
Left: BL top-left → TL bottom-left

Upload an image to start.
Four draggable AprilTags will appear at the image corners.
